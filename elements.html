About Me

Hi! I'm Han.

I am a Gameplay Programmer and Software Developer currently studying at the University of Melbourne. I've loved games for as long as I can remember, and that passion for games has driven me to pursue a career in making them! See my resume here!

My passion for games was sparked with the 2002 game Kingdom Hearts and I've been especially drawn to titles that deliver deeply engaging and satisfying player experiences like Command & Conquer 3, Outer Wilds, etc etc.

I started learning Godot and Unity and used them on my personal projects, and it's been a lot of fun (and a lot of debugging). I see myself as a problem solver that finds opportunities to learn new things, while constantly putting effort in improving my skills.

Outside of game development, I enjoy staying active and curious through my hobbies like volleyball, solo travel, hiking, and card games.
